# Phase 08 Gap Closure Plan 04: Fix Continuous Undo Deadlock When Human Plays Black

## Objective

Fix the continuous-undo deadlock that occurs when human plays Black. After undo restores a position where it's the AI's turn, the AI must be triggered to move.

**Root Cause (D-03 assumption violated):** Decision D-03 stated "悔棋后总是轮到红方（人类），无需 AI 重新走子逻辑". This is only true when human plays Red. When human plays Black and undoes a double-move, the turn correctly becomes Red (AI's turn), but `_start_ai_turn()` is never called to actually start the AI.

**Gap Source:** UAT Test 7 (blocker) - "continuous undo can deadlock AI when player is black"

## Bug Analysis

**Scenario: Human plays Black, AI responds, human undoes twice**

Sequence after human plays Black (`_human_side = -1`):
1. `new_game()`: AI plays Red first (because human is Black), turn = Black (-1), human to play
2. Human plays Black, turn = Red (+1), AI to play
3. AI plays Red, turn = Black (-1), human to play
4. Human clicks Undo (first undo):
   - `engine.undo()` removes AI's Red move, turn = Red (+1)
   - Condition `turn != _human_side` (+1 != -1) = True
   - Second `engine.undo()` removes human's Black move, turn = Black (-1)
5. After double-undo: `turn_changed.emit(-1)` fires
6. `_on_turn_changed(-1)`: `turn == _human_side` (-1 == -1) = True, board becomes interactive
7. **BUG**: No call to `_start_ai_turn()` - AI never plays, game deadlocked

**Correct behavior after fix:**
After step 4, if `turn == _ai_side` (AI's turn), call `_start_ai_turn()` to trigger AI.
