## Decisions Made

- Chariot geometry is purely orthogonal sliding: `(dr != 0) and (dc != 0)` → False immediately. In starting position, chariot at (9,0) is blocked right by horse at (9,1); only upward vertical slides are legal.

- The `_is_valid_geometry()` function is placed in `legal.py` alongside `_HORSE_LEG_DEST` (already there), keeping geometry tables co-located.
